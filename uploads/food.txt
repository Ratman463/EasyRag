🇬🇧 Traditional English Food Recipes
1. 🥧 Traditional Pie and Mash
A classic London dish dating back to the mid-19th century. Savory minced beef pies served with mashed potatoes and a green parsley sauce called "liquor." Originally made with eels by street pie men selling to working-class Londoners.

Prep: 30 min | Cook: 3 hrs | Serves: 6
Key Ingredients: Ground beef, beef stock, flour, butter, lard, Yukon Gold potatoes, fresh parsley
Highlight: Two different crusts — a flaky butter/lard top and a sturdier suet bottom crust.
2. 🥩 English Sunday Roast
The quintessential British meal, traditionally eaten after Sunday church. Features rosemary-roasted beef, carrots, parsnips, crispy duck-fat potatoes, creamy horseradish sauce, and warm gravy. Often accompanied by Yorkshire pudding.

Prep: 30 min | Cook: 1 hr 30 min | Serves: 6–8
Key Ingredients: Beef rump/round roast, Yukon Gold potatoes, carrots, parsnips, rosemary, duck fat, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce
Tip: Let beef rest 1–2 hours at room temp before roasting.
3. 🍖 Steak and Ale Pie
Tender braising steak slow-cooked in rich ale gravy, topped with golden puff pastry. A true British pub classic.

Prep: 25 min | Cook: 2 hrs | Serves: 4
Key Ingredients: Braising steak, brown ale, beef stock, onions, garlic, tomato purée, thyme, bay leaf, Worcestershire sauce, puff pastry
Tip: Cool the filling slightly before adding pastry to prevent a soggy bottom!
